About Top-Up and Get Reward (TGR)

Founded in November 2018, TGR is a fully NCC-licensed Telecoms marketing company that enables members to earn money by performing routine transactions, such as printed or VTU airtime and data purchases, cable TV subscriptions, and electricity bill payments. TGR’s business model empowers members to earn commissions from their own transactions as well as those of others within their network.

Members gain access to their own Virtual Top-Up (VTU) portal, where they can perform these transactions, build a team, and track their earnings. The more transactions performed and the larger the network grows, the greater the rewards.
